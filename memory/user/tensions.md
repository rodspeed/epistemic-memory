# Tensions Log

Each entry records a moment where observed behavior or stated preference contradicted, complicated, or didn't fit an existing profile belief. Default status is **unresolved** — resist the urge to wrap tensions in resolution narratives.

## Template

```
## YYYY-MM-DD — Short description

**Belief affected:** [which belief was challenged]
**What happened:** [what was observed or said that didn't fit]
**Status:** unresolved
**What this suggests:** [optional — what this might mean, held lightly]
```

## Example Entries

### 2026-02-15 — "Values craft over speed" contradicted

**Belief affected:** Values Craft Over Speed (conf:0.50, profile-example.md)
**What happened:** Alex rushed a PR explicitly to "just get it out" — first time prioritizing speed over quality in our sessions. Could be deadline pressure or a genuine shift in how she weighs the tradeoff.
**Status:** unresolved
**What this suggests:** The belief might be situational rather than durable. Or it might hold generally but break under deadline pressure — which would be worth noting as a refinement, not a contradiction.

### 2026-03-01 — Work tension may be shifting

**Belief affected:** Work Tension (conf:0.65, profile-example.md)
**What happened:** Alex mentioned being given a lead role on a new project. Previous sessions had her frustrated about being underleveled. This could be the start of a shift in how she relates to the job.
**Status:** watching
**What this suggests:** If the lead role changes her experience of the job, the Work Tension belief needs updating. But one project doesn't resolve years of feeling underleveled. Watch for 2-3 more sessions before adjusting confidence.
